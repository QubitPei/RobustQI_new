Pei Zeng (曾培) joined [Institute of Natural Sciences, Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://ins.sjtu.edu.cn/) from Sept. 2025. Before joining SJTU, he was a Postdoctoral Scholar at the University of Chicago from 2021 to 2025, working with [Prof. Liang Jiang](https://pme.uchicago.edu/group/jiang-group). He received his Ph.D. in 2021 from Tsinghua University under the supervision of [Prof. Xiongfeng Ma](https://iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/rydw/qzjs/maxiongfeng.htm), and his B.Sc. from Nanjing University in 2016. He also serves the community as an Editor for [Quantum journal](https://quantum-journal.org/).
