The **Robust Quantum Information (RobustQI) Group** at **Shanghai Jiao Tong University** focuses on building the theoretical foundations for reliable and scalable quantum technologies. Our research spans the following pillars:

* **Quantum Communication & Cryptography**: We design high-performance protocols, such as mode-pairing QKD, to surpass the linear rate-transmittance bound.
* **Quantum Error Correction (QEC)**: We develop fault-tolerant architectures and encoding schemes compatible with practical hardware, including bosonic qubits.
* **Quantum Algorithms**: We design efficient quantum algorithms for scientific computing, focusing on early fault-tolerant simulation and high-precision eigenstate problems.
* **Verification & Learning**: We work on efficient benchmarking and characterization of quantum states and channels to ensure the reliability of quantum devices.

# About the PI

**Pei Zeng (曾培)** is a Tenure-track Associate Professor at the [Institute of Natural Sciences, Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://ins.sjtu.edu.cn/). Before joining SJTU, he was a Postdoctoral Scholar at the **University of Chicago**, working with [Prof. Liang Jiang](https://pme.uchicago.edu/group/jiang-group). He received his Ph.D. from **Tsinghua University** under the supervision of [Prof. Xiongfeng Ma](https://iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/rydw/qzjs/maxiongfeng.htm), and his B.Sc. from **Nanjing University**. 
He also serves the community as an Editor for [Quantum journal](https://quantum-journal.org/).
